## Programmatic SEO Strategy

### The Opportunity
Create **templated, data-driven pages** at scale for high-intent keyword combinations that capture specific search queries. 46% of Google searches are local.

### Page Templates to Build

#### Template 1: City × Product Pages
**URL Pattern:** `/organic-waste-converter-[city]`
**Example:** `/organic-waste-converter-mumbai`
**Count:** 40-50 pages

**Template Structure:**
```
H1: Organic Waste Converter in [City] — Solwearth
- City waste statistics & regulations
- Which Solwearth product fits [city] facilities
- Local case studies / client references
- City-specific SWM compliance info
- Contact / quote form with city pre-selected
```

#### Template 2: Industry × Solution Pages
**URL Pattern:** `/[industry]-waste-management`
**Example:** `/hotel-waste-management`, `/hospital-waste-management`
**Count:** 10-15 pages

**Template Structure:**
```
H1: [Industry] Waste Management Solutions — Solwearth
- Industry-specific waste challenges
- Recommended products by capacity
- Industry case studies
- Regulatory requirements for this industry
- ROI data for this industry
- CTA: Industry-specific consultation
```

#### Template 3: City × Industry Pages (Advanced)
**URL Pattern:** `/[industry]-waste-management-[city]`
**Example:** `/hotel-waste-management-dubai`, `/apartment-waste-management-mumbai`
**Count:** 50-100 pages (start with highest value combos)

**Template Structure:**
```
H1: [Industry] Waste Management in [City] — Solwearth

- Local regulations affecting [Industry]
- Recommended solution for [Industry] in [City]
```

#### Template 4: Capacity × Application Pages
