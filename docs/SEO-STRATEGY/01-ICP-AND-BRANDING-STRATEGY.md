## Executive Summary

Solwearth Ecotech operates in a **$296.5M global OWC machine market** (2024) projected to reach **$574M by 2032** at 8.61% CAGR. India's waste management market alone will grow from $68.5B (2025) to $118.9B by 2033. The GCC waste management market is valued at $73.23B (2026) growing at 7.29% CAGR.

This document defines **10 distinct Ideal Customer Profiles**, maps their buying journeys, and establishes branding directions to position Solwearth as the dominant topical authority in organic waste management.

## Ideal Customer Profiles

### ICP #1: Hotel General Manager / Director of Operations

**DEMOGRAPHICS**
- **Title:** General Manager, Director of Operations, VP Operations
- **Industry:** Hospitality — Hotels & Resorts
- **Company Size:** 150-600 rooms; annual facility budget $2-8M
- **Age:** 38-55
- **Reports to:** Regional Director or Corporate Director of Operations
- **Geography:** India Tier-1 & Tier-2 cities, GCC countries (UAE, Saudi, Maldives)

**PSYCHOGRAPHICS**
- Values operational efficiency and cost control above all
- Increasingly pressured by corporate ESG mandates
- Concerned about regulatory penalties (SWM Rules 2016 mandate on-site processing for >100kg/day)
- Motivated by brand reputation and guest satisfaction scores
- Competitive — watches what peer hotels are doing

**PAIN POINTS**
1. Rising waste disposal costs (hauling, tipping fees, labor)
2. Space constraints for waste treatment in existing properties
3. Staff turnover makes consistent waste segregation difficult
4. Regulatory compliance pressure (SWM Rules 2016, municipal mandates)
5. Need for green certifications (IGBC, LEED, Green Key) for corporate/government bookings
6. Guest complaints about odor/hygiene from waste areas

**BUYING BEHAVIOR**
- **Discovery:** LinkedIn, Hotelier India, peer referrals, industry conferences (ISHP, India International Hotel Expo)
- **Evaluation:** ROI-focused — needs to see payback period <2 years
- **Decision Committee:** GM + Facilities Manager/Chief Engineer + Finance Manager + (for chains) Corporate Sustainability Officer
- **Timeline:** Independent hotels: 3-6 months | Chain properties: 9-18 months
- **Budget:** $30,000-$150,000 depending on property size

**SEARCH KEYWORDS**

- "SWM Rules compliance hotels"
- "hotel sustainability solutions"
- "food waste disposal cost reduction hotel"
- "composting machine for hotel kitchen"
- "hotel waste management Mumbai/Delhi/Dubai"

**KEY MESSAGING**
> "Reduce waste disposal costs by 40-60% while achieving full regulatory compliance. Join Marriott, Hilton, Taj, and 50+ hotels already using Solwearth."

**BEST CONTENT FORMATS**
